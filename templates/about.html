About MARLO

MARLO is a clean, fast content management platform built for writers who care about their readers. Publish, engage, and track everything — all in one place.

✍️

Write freely

Distraction-free writing with full media support — images, files, and rich content all in one editor.

💬

Engage your audience

Readers can comment, like, and interact. Built-in moderation tools keep your community healthy.

📊

Track performance

Read counts, likes, and comment metrics at a glance straight from your admin dashboard.

🔒

Secure by default

JWT-authenticated API, CSRF protection, session security, and role-based access control built in.
